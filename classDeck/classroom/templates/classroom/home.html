About

Now-a-days Traditional method of teaching are replaced by virtual or online sessions. In these sessions, tutors and learners are interconnected through the internet, and the online learning environment is called virtual classrooms. Students connect with teachers anywhere, anytime through interactive online platform virtually. New tools like online Assignments & Tests, video conferencing are needs for this new-age learning method. Virtual classrooms break the synchronous hurdles of location, place, student limits and cater the knowledge to the learning crowd.
